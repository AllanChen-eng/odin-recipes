Banana Smoothie

Return to main page

Description

Tasty, refreshing, and delicious treat for the summer!

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Ice cubes

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients.

  2. Place banana, milk, peanut butter, honey and ice cubes in a blender

  3. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds

  4. Enjoy!
